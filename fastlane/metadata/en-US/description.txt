Quickly find bike sharing stations in cities across North America. With support for 6 cities you only need one application instead of one per city.

Bike Share Buddy supports the following services(cities):
- Bike Chattanooga (Chattanooga, TN)
- Divvy (Chicago, IL)
- CoGo (Columbus, OH)
- Citi Bike (New York City, NY)
- Bay Area Bike Share (San Francisco, CA)
- Bike Share Toronto (Toronto, Ontario Canada)
